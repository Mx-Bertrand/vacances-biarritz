1. Description

Biarritz, or Miarritze in basque, is a city in the South-West of France, located in the Bay of Biscay, near Spain. The mild climate of the resort, the spectacle of the ocean and its powerful waves, as well as the eclecticism of the architecture have inspired many writers or chroniclers.
